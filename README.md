# CareerCapital AI   
AI-powered platform to help students plan education, careers, and finances smarter.

## Problem
Students struggle with choosing the right university, understanding loans, and planning finances, 
leading to poor decisions and financial stress.

## Solution
CareerCapital AI provides AI-driven recommendations for universities, financial planning tools, and 
income-based EMI predictions to guide students end-to-end.
